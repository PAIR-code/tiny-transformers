info How does it work?

We train a character-level model (like Kaparthy's Shakespeare next-character predictor) directly in your browser. Characters are embedded as 5 Berkovich disks $(c_i, \rho_i) \in \Gamma_p^5$. Next token predictions are computed by taking the aggregated context representation and projecting it against learned affinoid domains (constraints) for each alphabet class using the Affinoid Softmax:

Where $M_{{ '{' }}k,d{{ '}' }}$ is the continuous path-metric error on the tree. Regularization penalizes the disk radii ($p^\rho$), creating a continuous separation margin and resolving prediction ties.
